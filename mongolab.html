mLab MongoDB

Cloud-hosted MongoDB Starting at $0/mo.

The Magic of the Cloud

Create databases and replica set clusters on-demand. Scale up and down with ease. Never think about machines and never install software. We give you a connection string, and you are good to go!
Optimal MongoDB Performance with SSDs

Enjoy 100% SSD-backed disks and dedicated MongoDB processes on all of our for-pay plans.
Maximum Uptime

We monitor every database in our fleet 24x7 and always have a team of expert database administrators on-call to make sure your database stays up and healthy. Our replica set cluster plans offer high availability across multiple availability zones.
Expert Care and Support

Get advice from the expert team that manages and optimizes over half a million databases worldwide. Thoughtful, timely support from real developers is why users love us.
Total Data Protection

Your data is safe with mLab. We offer flexible, multi-zone replication options as well as the ability to create recurring backup plans (hourly/daily/weekly/monthly) and initiate one-time snapshots to any S3 bucket.
Web-based Database Management

Our admin tools allow you to search, visualize, and modify data with ease as well as help you optimize your queries and indexes. We guarantee that you’ll love these tools and that you won’t be able to live without them.
