Storj

Distributed object storage Starting at $0/mo.

Built In Speed

The Storj network is designed for speed. When retrieving your files, you are able to download multiple pieces at the same time similar to a torrent. This makes your downloads faster than ever!
Easy Implementation

Using the Storj platform is simple and straightforward. With plenty of available documentation and examples, you can get started in no time.
Easy Setup, Now with Videos

We created a video tutorial series just for you, to help you integrate the Storj add-on with your Heroku application here.
Effortless Encryption

Files uploaded to Storj are encrypted by default allowing you to rest assured that your data is safe and secure.
