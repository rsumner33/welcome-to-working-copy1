openredis

Dependable Redis Hosting. Starting at $8/mo.

Super easy setup

Provisioning an instance with openredis is fast and easy. We handle all the heavy lifting for you.
Scale with Ease

Choose a plan which suits your needs, and upgrade to a bigger plan anytime.
Automated Backups

Data storage is always a pain. You have to make sure that you run backups and that backups are always working.
Memory monitoring

Hosting Redis requires careful handholding. One of the most common problems people encounter is running out of memory. With openredis, we monitor your memory, and notify you when you reach 90% usage. In addition, we boost your max memory temporarily in order to prevent potential data loss.
Disaster recovery plans

All plans include automatic failover. For cases when we expect downtime from huge catastrophic events, we plan ahead and setup temporary failover instances in different regions just to make sure that you don’t lose data.
Redundancy

Our mission is to provide the most reliable hosting service out there. All plans include 1 slave automatically. Should your main instance go down, your backup slave can be promoted as the new master, and redundancy will be restored in seconds.
Latest and Greatest

We stay updated with the latest in Redis, and use version 3.2.7 for all new instances provisioned. In some cases when our customers need to live on the edge, we have no problem setting their instance up to use the latest unstable version.
24/7 Support

Our focus on reliability is tied to our dedication for offering the best customer service. We are commited to help you whenever you need us.
