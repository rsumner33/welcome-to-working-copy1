GrapheneDB

Neo4j Graph Database as a Service Starting at $0/mo.

Hassle-free, one-click Neo4j hosting

Deploy Neo4j graph databases instantly. Do not waste time managing, installing and configuring servers.
Backups and data safety

All plans feature manual restore and export. Production plans feature scheduled hot backups and on-demand snapshots. All plans feature SSL encrypted network connections.
Multi-language Neo4j support

Connect to your instances via Bolt or HTTP REST using standard drivers. Check out our docs for a comprehensive guide on different languages and frameworks.
24/7 health checks & engineers on-call

Our 24x7 monitoring system proactively detects and reports anomalies for you. Our expert engineering team on-call keeps your instance running around-the-clock.
24x7 Expert technical support

All plan levels include email support from our expert team. From driver configuration to query and database optimization, weâ€™re here to help.
Tools to help you succeed

Troubleshoot performance bottlenecks and identify slow queries with our Insights dashboard. Extend your database with unmanaged servers extensions and stored procedures.
